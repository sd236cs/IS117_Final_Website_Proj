Pizza-like Specialties

"I'd rather have leftover pizza than leftover feelings"

Quiche

A French dish that resembles pizza. It often features the same ingredients as pizza, like meat, cheese, and vegetables.

Nutella Pizza

May sound absurd, but nutella pizza is a thing! And it's delicious too!

Pissaladiere

Another French pizza-like dish. The original recipe uses only 3 toppings: bacon, caramelized onions, and olives.

Sushi Pizza

A mashup of two totally different world cuisines. Looks like pizza, tastes like sushi.

Calzone

Aka closed pizza. Features all classic pizza toppings but is baked closed.

Focaccia

Italian flatbread topped with tomatoes, basil, and olive oil. No cheese here...

Stromboli

Similar to calzone. The difference is that the dough for stromboli is purposefully made too wide, so toppings can be rolled and baked as a roll.
